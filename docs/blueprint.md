# **App Name**: Zambian Canvas

## Core Features:

- Artist & Buyer Accounts: Allow users to register, log in securely, and manage distinct profiles as either artists selling their work or buyers seeking art.
- Art Exhibition & Discovery: Provide a browseable catalog where users can view Zambian artworks with detailed descriptions, high-quality images, and artist portfolios.
- Artwork Submission & Listing: Enable registered artists to easily upload their original art, specify details like medium, dimensions, price, and manage their collection.
- Secure Art Acquisition: Implement a straightforward and secure process for buyers to purchase artworks directly from artists on the platform.
- Personalized Art Suggestions (AI tool): An AI tool that suggests artworks and artists to users based on their browsing history, preferences, and aesthetic interactions, to foster an 'artistic and African inspired' environment.
- Direct Artist-Buyer Messaging: A simple in-app messaging system to facilitate communication and inquiries between interested buyers and artists.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Rich earthy orange (#D97326) to evoke the warmth and natural tones of the Zambian landscape and artistry, acting as a vibrant anchor.
- Background color: A soft, warm cream (#F7F0E8) to provide an inviting canvas that makes the artwork pop and enhances readability in a light scheme.
- Accent color: A deep, striking red (#BF3131), analogous to the primary color, used for calls to action, highlights, and to inject additional vibrancy and emphasis.
- Headline font: 'Alegreya' (humanist serif) for an elegant, artistic, and intellectual feel that resonates with artistic expression.
- Body font: 'Inter' (grotesque sans-serif) for its modern, clean lines, ensuring high readability for art descriptions and user interface elements.
- Utilize icons that draw inspiration from simplified African art motifs, geometric patterns, and elements of nature, maintaining a clean and modern aesthetic.
- Adopt a grid-based, adaptive layout for artwork display, allowing for flexible card sizes to create an engaging visual flow akin to an art gallery, emphasizing individual pieces with generous spacing.
- Incorporate subtle and smooth transitions for loading images, navigating between art pieces, and profile interactions to enhance the fluid and artistic user experience.
